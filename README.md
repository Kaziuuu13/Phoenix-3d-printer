# Phoenix-3d-printer
  About 4 years ago I have made myself a small 3d printer from scrach. During those years printer started showing it's age. It jams almost every print, and beacuse of that trying to get acceptable quality print from it is a nightmare. 
  During those 4 years i have not done a lot of projects beside few small things, and I belive that this one will, be perfect to get started with robotics again after such long break(I must admit that I don't feel as confident making thigs as I used too. I guess I'm just rusty, and that's all)

  
  Whole idea is to design and assemble a working 3d printer using easly accesible parts e.g. using angle brackets instead of 3d printed stepper motor mounts. After I am done with initial design I will be able to print these parts myself and keep on upgrading my 3d printer's capabilities. 
I will be documenting progres in the JOURNAL.md in this repo. My goal is to make this project as easy to modify or replicate by anyone who would want to build anything similar in the future. 





I'll keep on updating the README.md accordingly to the progress.
